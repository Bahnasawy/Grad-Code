Problem:
	Authorship attribution of the Enron email dataset containing 87 authors.

Solution:
	Using stylometric techniques through the analysis of linguistic styles and writing characteristics.

Algorithm Name:
	Supervised learning and n-gram analysis.

Main Findings:
	The experiment yielded an EER(Estimated Error Rate) of 14.35%

Pros and Cons:
	Pros: It has achieved a better EER than other research papers with a lesser character count.
	Cons: It still falls short when investigating shorter and unrelated texts like twitter messages.
