Problem:
	Authorship attribution with a limited number of characters like twitter's 140 characters limit causing a scarcity of information, but having and enormous overall corpus.

Solution:
	Computing very low-level lexical statistics

Algorithm Name:
	PMSVM (Power Mean Support Vector Machine)

Main finidings:
	They were able to achieve an accuracy of 70% for 50 authors.

Pros and Cons:
	Pro: The proposed method can be used for search-space reduction or helping to assemble more conventional evidence.
	Con: It is still an incomplete process and researchers still nee to look at the graphical structure of the social network to be able to make a more accurate decision.
